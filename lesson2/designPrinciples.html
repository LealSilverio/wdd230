Hick's Law

UNIQLO

uniqlo.com

Even though UNIQLO sells a lot of different pieces of clothing, they manage to use slide galleries to make sure there are not too many elements on the screen. That way you can scroll as much as you want and not be overwhelmed with the amount of information.

Repetition

Instagram

instagram.com

The website uses a lot of repetition to make sure the user knows what to expect. The stories follows the same patters (circular profile picture with colored border), as well as the feed (profile information followed by post and coments).

Contrast

YouTube

youtube.com

The website uses a limited color pallete with high contrast: black, white, and red. There are some variations like the use of gray, but there is contrast. Even in their add they use high contrast to call the users attention.
