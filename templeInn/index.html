São Paulo Brazil Temple

About it

The São Paulo Brazil Temple was the first temple built in South America. After the dedication of the São Paulo Brazil Temple there were 41 additional stakes were organized over the next 12 years, putting national membership at more than 300,000. Before the São Paulo Brazil Temple was constructed, the closest temple to the Brazilian Saints was the Washington D.C. Temple.
